## 📌 Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world e-commerce business database built
entirely from scratch using **synthetic data generated in MySQL**.
The project covers everything from database design, table creation,
data insertion to writing complex business queries with **JOINs, CTEs,
Window Functions, and CASE WHEN** logic — demonstrating production-level
SQL skills.

## 💼 Business Queries & Insights

---

### 🔍 Query 1 — Total Revenue Generated

> **Business Question:** What is the total revenue generated by the company from all orders?

```sql
SELECT 
    SUM(p.price * oi.quantity) AS total_revenue
FROM order_items oi
JOIN products p 
ON oi.product_id = p.product_id;
```

💡 **Business Insight:** This metric shows the overall financial performance
of the business. Tracking total revenue helps management evaluate growth
trends and make strategic decisions about pricing, promotions, and inventory.

---

### 🔍 Query 2 — Top 5 Best Selling Products

> **Business Question:** Which products generate the highest revenue for the business?

```sql
SELECT 
    p.product_name,
    SUM(oi.quantity) AS total_units_sold
FROM order_items oi
JOIN products p ON oi.product_id = p.product_id
GROUP BY p.product_name
ORDER BY total_units_sold DESC
LIMIT 5;
```

💡 **Business Insight:** Understanding top-performing products helps businesses
focus on inventory planning, marketing efforts, and supply chain optimization
to maximize profit.

---

### 🔍 Query 3 — Top 10 High Value Customers

> **Business Question:** Which customers contribute the most revenue to the company?

```sql
SELECT 
    c.customer_name,
    SUM(p.price * oi.quantity) AS total_spent
FROM customers c
JOIN orders o ON c.customer_id = o.customer_id
JOIN order_items oi ON o.order_id = oi.order_id
JOIN products p ON oi.product_id = p.product_id
GROUP BY c.customer_name
ORDER BY total_spent DESC
LIMIT 10;
```

💡 **Business Insight:** A small percentage of customers usually generate a
large portion of revenue (Pareto Principle). Identifying these high-value
customers helps businesses design loyalty programs and targeted marketing campaigns.

---

### 🔍 Query 4 — Monthly Revenue Trend

> **Business Question:** How does revenue change month by month?

```sql
SELECT 
    DATE_FORMAT(o.order_date,'%Y-%m') AS month,
    SUM(p.price * oi.quantity) AS revenue
FROM orders o
JOIN order_items oi ON o.order_id = oi.order_id
JOIN products p ON oi.product_id = p.product_id
GROUP BY month
ORDER BY month;
```

💡 **Business Insight:** Monthly revenue trends help identify seasonality
patterns, sales growth, and potential slow periods. Businesses can use
this to plan promotions and marketing strategies during low-performing months.

---

### 🔍 Query 5 — Customer Segmentation (CASE WHEN)

> **Business Question:** How can we categorize customers based on their total spending?

```sql
SELECT 
    c.customer_id,
    c.customer_name,
    SUM(p.price * oi.quantity) AS total_spent,
    CASE 
        WHEN SUM(p.price * oi.quantity) > 500 THEN 'High Value Customer'
        WHEN SUM(p.price * oi.quantity) BETWEEN 200 AND 500 THEN 'Medium Value Customer'
        ELSE 'Low Value Customer'
    END AS customer_segment
FROM customers c
JOIN orders o ON c.customer_id = o.customer_id
JOIN order_items oi ON o.order_id = oi.order_id
JOIN products p ON oi.product_id = p.product_id
GROUP BY c.customer_id, c.customer_name;
```

💡 **Business Insight:** Customer segmentation helps businesses identify their
most valuable customers and design personalized marketing campaigns for each group.

---

### 🔍 Query 6 — Customer Lifetime Value (CTE)

> **Business Question:** Which customers generate the highest lifetime revenue?

```sql
WITH customer_spending AS (
    SELECT 
        c.customer_id,
        c.customer_name,
        SUM(p.price * oi.quantity) AS lifetime_value
    FROM customers c
    JOIN orders o ON c.customer_id = o.customer_id
    JOIN order_items oi ON o.order_id = oi.order_id
    JOIN products p ON oi.product_id = p.product_id
    GROUP BY c.customer_id, c.customer_name
)
SELECT * FROM customer_spending
ORDER BY lifetime_value DESC;
```

💡 **Business Insight:** Identifying high CLV customers helps businesses focus
on retention strategies, loyalty programs, and premium personalized services.

---

### 🔍 Query 7 — Product Revenue Ranking (Window Function)

> **Business Question:** Which products generate the highest revenue with rankings?

```sql
SELECT 
    p.product_id,
    p.product_name,
    SUM(p.price * oi.quantity) AS total_revenue,
    RANK() OVER (
        ORDER BY SUM(p.price * oi.quantity) DESC
    ) AS product_rank
FROM products p
JOIN order_items oi ON p.product_id = oi.product_id
GROUP BY p.product_id, p.product_name;
```

💡 **Business Insight:** Identifying top-ranked products helps businesses focus
on inventory planning, marketing campaigns, and pricing strategy optimization.
